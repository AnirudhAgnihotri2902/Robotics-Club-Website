    Competitions

    Robotics Club represents Ajay Kumar Garg Engineering College in various National and International competitions

    ABU Robocon

    The ABU Asia-Pacific Robot Contest is an Asian Oceanian College robot competition, founded in 2002 by Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union.

    Mitsubishi Electric Cup

    Participating teams are required to independently design and produce a complete set of devices/models by using mitsubishi electric factory automation products.

    World Robot Olympiad

    Starting in 2004, World Robot Olympiad has developed to a truly global competition. WRO tournaments are organized in more than 60 countries.

    NI-Yantra

    NIYANTRA is an annual System Design contest organized by National Instruments to foster the spirit of innovation in engineering students.

    Technovation

    The event is planned with different theme based technological challenges to facilitate participation from different categories of talent.

    Smart India Hackathon

    It is a national level technical event conducted by the govt of India. This event is a another push to digital India initiative.

    Technologies

    This section includes all the technologies that we use in our robots to perform any perticular task.

    Cad Designing

    Before we start to fabricate the final bot, we need to first design and simulate the whole mechnical structure first.

    Image Processing

    The power of image processing can replace all the sensors. You will watch the live example of image processing in Bot Show.

    Embedded Systems

    Embedded systems are heart of our robots. We use arduino and raspberry pi in our bots to perform tasks.

    Pneumatics

    Simplicity of design and control, Reliability and Safety all these factors make pneumatics a powerfull tool for our machines.

    Sensors

    We use ultrasonic, proximity, gyroscope, etc. Including line following sensors LSA08 in our robots.

    Electronics

    Electronics is a sub-discipline of engineering and allow you to specialize in designing devices and system based on printed circuit board.
