    RoboRace

    AKGEC Robotics Club presents RoboRace Competitions

    Game description

    The task is to build a robot either wired or wireless within the specified dimensions to achieve the maximum speed to beat other robots on the given track and reach the finishing line in minimum time. There will be two trial runs and on the basis of the fastest times and points earned from hurdles, teams will be selected for the final round.

    Rounds

    Each team will be given three chances to complete the racecourse and the time for each round will be noted. after every team has completed the race least time taken from the provided three chances will be considered and the top 3 teams will be awarded.

    Arena Specification

    Dimensions: 20X20 ft2
    Track length: 80 feet

    Specification of the track

    The track will be an elongated shape spread from edge to edge from start to finish.

    Track Dimension

    Minimum width 40 cm
    Maximum width 60 cm
    Maximum height of Hurdle 20 cm from base.
